5.1 Publishing to Power BI Service

Uploading Reports and Datasets

After creating your reports in Power BI Desktop, the next step is publishing them to the Power BI Service. Here's how to do it:

  1. Publishing a Report:
    • In Power BI Desktop, click "Publish" and select a workspace in the Power BI Service.
    • The report is uploaded to the cloud, making it accessible online.
  2. Publishing a Dataset:
    • You can publish datasets (data models) independently of reports, making them available for multiple reports.
    • Go to the Power BI Service, select a workspace, and choose "Get Data" to connect to a published dataset.

Sharing and Collaboration

Sharing reports with others and collaborating in real-time are essential aspects of using the Power BI Service.

  1. Sharing Reports:
    • Share reports with others by publishing them to workspaces.
    • Specify who can view or edit the report by configuring sharing settings.
  2. Collaboration:
    • Collaborate in real-time with team members on shared reports.
    • Use comments and discussions to communicate and provide feedback.

5.2 Dashboards and Reports

Building Interactive Dashboards

Interactive dashboards in the Power BI Service enable you to create a unified view of your data and reports.

  1. Creating Dashboards:
    • Dashboards allow you to combine multiple visuals from different reports into one view.
    • Pin visuals from various reports to a dashboard to create a unified experience.
  2. Adding Tiles:
    • To add tiles to a dashboard, click "Pin Live Page" or "Pin to Dashboard" in the Power BI Service.
    • You can pin visuals, images, text boxes, and more.

Managing Content in the Power BI Service

Efficiently manage your Power BI content and ensure data freshness in the Power BI Service.

  1. Organizing Workspaces:
    • Workspaces help you organize content by project, team, or department.
    • You can create workspaces and manage permissions for members.
  2. Publishing Reports to a Workspace:
    • When publishing reports from Power BI Desktop, select the appropriate workspace in the Power BI Service.
  3. Refresh Schedules:
    • Configure automatic data refresh schedules for your datasets to keep your reports up-to-date.
